Introducing My Site

My name is Patricio Spamer, but my friends call me Pato, which means duck in spanish. I received a Bachelor of Business Administration in Finance from the Cox School of Business, and a Bachelor of Science in Economics with Financial Applications from Dedman college, both schools in Southern Methodist University. I am now seeking to gain a background in technology so that I will better my ability to add value to my fathers logistics and industrial real estate firm. I am hoping that by gaining this background in tech, that I will be able to bring technological innovation to a very traditional but successful bricks and mortar company that is CIL logistics.

I believe that the best way to add value to my fathers firm will be to recruit and manage a high quality software team that will help me create and implement innovative technologies that will take us from being a small company of roughly 100 employees to a fast growing company of 500+ employees. In addition to work that we will be doing for the family business, I want this software team to help me implement some of my own startup ideas that are not related to logistics or real estate. I believe my greatest asset is my sense of vision and perspective, and I hope to combine these skills with technical expertise to become a high capacity businessman.

I am also an angel investor in technology startups, my first investment being in Irys, a urban-centric technology platform from San Antonio.
